  • Lasagna

    Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with rich, savory fillings such as seasoned ground meat, creamy béchamel sauce, tomato-based marinara, and melted cheese — typically mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan. Baked to golden perfection, lasagna is known for its comforting, hearty texture and bold flavors that blend with each layer. While traditional versions focus on meat-based fillings, variations like vegetarian, seafood, or even vegan lasagna have become popular, making it a versatile and beloved meal across cultures. Whether served at family dinners or festive gatherings, lasagna remains a timeless comfort food favorite.

  • Schezwan Noodles

    Schezwan Noodles are a popular Indo-Chinese dish known for their bold, spicy, and tangy flavors. Made by stir-frying noodles with a variety of vegetables like bell peppers, cabbage, and carrots, they are tossed in a fiery Schezwan sauce—a chili garlic-based sauce originating from the Sichuan region of China, adapted to Indian tastes.

    This dish is characterized by its vibrant red color and intense, smoky aroma. It's a favorite street food in India and often served as a main course or a side with Manchurian or chili paneer. Schezwan Noodles strike a perfect balance between heat, umami, and slight sweetness, making them a must-try for spice lovers and fans of Asian fusion cuisine.
